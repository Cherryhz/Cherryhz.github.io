Spanish holiday accommodation pros and cons

Villa near Alicante Pros and Cons

There is a direct flight from Stansted to Alicante. The table will summarise the villas I have found, all of which have private pools. Driving time are all from Alicante.

Villa link Location Price (1 week) Pros Cons
Villa Kenchico 1h drive £1145
  • check-in time slightly earlier at 2pm
  • big pool and nice bar area
  • near Montgo mountain and the sea
  • quiet surroundings
  • décor looks a bit old
  • need to bring beach/pool towels
  • 20min drive to beach
  • change-over day needs to be Saturday from 4pm, so not very flexible
Villa Casa Del Sol 40min drive £1368
  • right in front of a beach, take 20min walk to Calpe
  • Very nice view
  • a lot of bars and restaurants on the beach
  • separate studio/bedroom for different families
  • playroom for children
  • maybe noisy as lots of bars around
Villa Filipinas 50min drive £2061
  • décor looks quite new
  • review said there is a bus service to the beach, so could do without hiring a car
  • lots of amenities nearby
  • A lot of houses around, so may overlook the pool and it looks a little cramp.
  • bit far out without a car
  • not as spacious as others
Galatea Rural 50min drive via a troll £2115
  • décor looks quite new and the garden is relaxing
  • quiet but natural surroundings
  • landlord lives in the same building but in a separate apartment
  • good bed sizes (quite a few king size beds)
  • only one review
  • limited electricity use of 300 kwh per week
Ciudad Quesada 40min drive £1534
  • décor looks quite new and the garden is relaxing
  • game room, jacuzzi and big bar area
  • close to amenities and nature walk is also 5min away
  • all rooms are en-suite
  • good reviews and owners can arrange transfer and may not need car
  • residential area looks quite cramp
  • may need to ask for bikes when going to supermarket
Vallenaranjos complex 40min drive £1742
  • in the forest with two pools, jacuzzi and looks a little like a tree house
  • quiet nature setting
  • 17km from beach
  • not sure about amenities around

Villa near Barcelona/Sitges Pros and Cons

There is a direct flight from Stansted to Barcelona. The table will summarise the villas I have found, all of which have private pools. Driving time are all from Barcelona. One plus point for Barcelona is Olly can drive there and we can rent 2 5-seater which may lower the price.

Villa link Location Price (1 week) Pros Cons
Villa Can Suria 40min drive £1743
  • modern décor
  • pool towels provided
  • fencing around the swimming pool for extra security
  • sounds like it's agent run, need to check-in at Sitges office
  • pool looks quite narrow
  • only has fans on top floor, no air-con
  • one bed short (for a kid)
  • need a car, but short drive to Sitges, Barcelona
Villa Antonia 40min drive £1630
  • décor looks ok
  • big pool
  • enough bed for everyone
  • on top of the hill so good view, nearby Mataró beach is quite nice
  • pool side is not very big
  • not very big outdoor sitting area
  • 5-10min drive to local shops and restaurants
Cabrera de Mar Villa 40min drive £1435
  • quite a few attractions, beach, ruined castle
  • in the mountain park so quite natural
  • enough bed for everyone
  • nice children play area
  • decor is outdated
  • no review, although quite recently started to rent out
  • maybe hard to drive to
Villa Ananda 55min drive £1865
  • walking distance to amenities and beach
  • decor quite contemporary
  • enough bed for everyone
  • very big pool
  • car not necessary, taxi cost 75euro one way
  • no review, although quite recently started to rent out
Vilanova i la Geltru Villa 20min drive £1289
  • easy access to amenities and train station to Barcelona (30min train)
  • nice garden, pool and children play area
  • enough bed for everyone
  • secure after closing the automatic gate
  • walking to seafront at Cubelles is easy
  • car is essential
